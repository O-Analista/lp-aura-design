# Project Brief: Studio Aura Landing Page

## 1. Project Overview
**Client:** Marina Vasconcelos, Studio Aura
**Industry:** High-End Interior Design & Architecture
**Objective:** Create an elegant, sophisticated landing page that serves as an immersive digital experience for a luxury interior design studio based in São Paulo.

## 2. Brand Identity & Visual Direction
- **Brand Name:** Studio Aura
- **Concept:** "Aura Noir" — Discrete luxury, boutique hotel aesthetic, art gallery feel.
- **Visual Style:** Sophisticated, minimalist ("less is more"), high-contrast, attention to detail.
- **Color Palette:**
  - Primary: Deep Graphite / Absolute Black (#131313)
  - Accent: Metallic Gold Leaf (#D4AF37)
  - Text: Silk White / Soft Grey
- **Typography:** Playfair Display (Serif) for headlines to convey elegance; clean sans-serif for body copy.

## 3. Core Features & Structure
1.  **Hero Section:** Immersive opening with a background video (`interior-dark-video.mp4`) and a minimalist headline.
2.  **Process Section:** "Nossa Essência em 3 Etapas" (Concept, Curation, Delivery) featuring integrated video (`video-camadas.mp4`).
3.  **Portfolio:** Showcase of 6-7 high-end interior projects with varying aspect ratios.
4.  **About Us:** Personal section for Marina Vasconcelos featuring her story and professional photography.
5.  **Testimonials:** Social proof from high-profile clients.
6.  **Contact & Lead Gen:** Minimalist contact form and a direct WhatsApp integration.
7.  **Footer:** Essential links, social media (Instagram, LinkedIn), and copyright.

## 4. Technical Specifications
- **Device Target:** Mobile-first (Responsive).
- **Design System:** Aura Noir Custom System (CSS Variables, Gold accents, Tonal layering).
- **Interactions:** Smooth transitions, backdrop blurs, and hover effects to maintain a "luxury" feel.

## 5. Assets Provided
- Hero background video.
- Process assembly video.
- 7 Project portfolio images.
